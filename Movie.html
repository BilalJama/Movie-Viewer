Canadian actor Ryan Gosling first found success by joining the cast of The Mickey Mouse Club in 1993. As an adult, he starred in the romantic hit The Notebook (2004) and garnered award nominations for Half Nelson (2006) and Lars and the Real Girl (2007). Gosling has also appeared in Blue Valentine (2010), Crazy, Stupid, Love (2011), The Ides of March (2011) and The Big Short (2015), among other projects. He received Golden Globe and Oscar nominations for his portrayal of a jazz pianist in the 2016 hit movie musical La La Land, and the following year he starred in the popular sci-fi flick Blade Runner 2049.
